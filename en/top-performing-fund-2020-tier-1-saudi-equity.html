Best-Performing Tier-1 Fund 2020

NCB Capital’s AlAhli Small and Mid-Cap Fund named ‘Best-Performing Tier-1 Fund 2020’ by Argaam

NCB Capital’s AlAhli Small and Mid-Cap Equity Fund won Argaam Recognition Award as “Best Performing Tier 1 Fund of the Year 2020”.

AlAhli Small and Mid-Cap Equity Fund was the top performer among Tier 1 funds in 2020, with net assets under management (AUM) of more than SAR 100 million. The fund achieved an annual return of 22.03%.

Argaam conducted a study on mutual funds that invest in Saudi stocks exclusively. These vehicles were divided into two categories: Tier 1 funds with net assets above SAR 100 million and Tier 2 funds with net assets below SAR 100 million.

AlAhli Small and Mid-Cap Equity Fund seeks to generate long-term capital growth through small and mid-cap companies listed in the Saudi equity market in compliance with Sharia.

The fund invests in various Saudi-listed equities. AlAhli Saudi Riyal Trade Fund accounts for 14.86% of the fund’s total investment. AlAhli Freestyle Saudi Equity Fund came second with 9.07% of total investments.

Argaam Recognition Award aims to honor mutual funds with the highest investment return in various investment terms. It also seeks to support excellence, boost transparency, and encourage market participants to target the highest performance.
