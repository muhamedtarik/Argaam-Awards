Ashmore Saudi Equity Fund named ‘Best-Performing Tier-2 Fund in 5 Years’ by Argaam

Ashmore Saudi Equity Fund, managed by Ashmore Investment Saudi Arabia, won Argaam Recognition Award as “Best Performing Tier 2 Fund in 5 Years”.

Ashmore Saudi Equity Fund was the best performer among Tier 2 funds in five years, with net assets under management (AUM) of no more than SAR 100 million. The fund achieved an annual return of 70.4%.

Argaam conducted a study on mutual funds that invest in Saudi stocks exclusively. These vehicles were divided into two categories: Tier 1 funds with net assets above SAR 100 million and Tier 2 funds with net assets below SAR 100 million.

The primary objective of Ashmore Saudi Equity Fund is to provide medium to long-term capital growth by investing in a diversified portfolio of shares of companies listed on the Saudi Stock Exchange (Tadawul), including IPOs or rights issues.

Argaam Recognition Award aims to honor mutual funds with the highest investment return in various investment terms. It also seeks to support excellence, boost transparency, and encourage market participants to target the highest performance.
