Derayah Financial named ‘Best Brokerage Company in Customer Service 2020’ by Argaam

Derayah Financial was recognized by Argaam Investment Co. as “Best Brokerage Company in Customer Service of the Year 2020”, according to the survey conducted by the financial online portal “Argaam”.

Argaam Recognition Award aims to honor mutual funds with the highest investment return in various investment terms. It also seeks to support excellence, boost transparency, and encourage market participants to target the highest performance.
